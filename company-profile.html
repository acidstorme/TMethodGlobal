TMethod Global Private Limited

TMethod Global Private Limited helps international aviation companies set up and scale their operations in India. Strategically based at Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGI), New Delhi, we combine local execution with global standards across Aviation Consulting, HR Consulting, and Office Space Management.

What We Do

  • Enable India entry and operational readiness for aviation stakeholders
  • Provide regulatory liaison and airport coordination support
  • Build compliant teams through structured HR consulting
  • Manage serviced office space solutions near IGI Airport

Why It Works

  • Location advantage: operations proximity at IGI Airport
  • Compliance-first processes and documentation discipline
  • Dedicated staffing and managed services engagement options
  • Transparent reporting and stakeholder coordination

Cargo & Logistics

Cargo Operations Process Optimization

  • Analysis of current cargo handling procedures to identify operational gaps and implement improvements through technology integration, process standardization, and efficiency-driven operational enhancements.

Airside Logistics Support

  • Supply and management of cargo vehicles for airside operations, facilitating smooth cargo movement between aircraft, cargo terminals, and airport facilities.

Courier & Air Cargo Logistics Services

  • Courier Handling: End-to-end management of courier shipments including acceptance, processing, and coordination with airline cargo operations.
  • Air Cargo Space Coordination: Arrangement and confirmation of cargo space on outbound flights for export shipments.
  • Courier Customs Clearance: Facilitation of customs documentation and clearance to ensure compliant and timely export processing.
  • Competitive Tariff: Access to cost-effective and competitive freight tariffs through strong coordination with airline and logistics partners.

Regulatory Liaison

  • Support for Schedule, Non Schedule Operations (NSOP), and overflying permissions
  • Coordination with Airports Authority of India (AAI)
  • Compliance and risk management advisory

Airport & Slot Management
